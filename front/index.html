CHICKEN BIRYANI

Author: Rani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cleaned)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala, turmeric, chili powder
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves)
  • Coriander & mint leaves
  • Salt and oil/ghee

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste. Rest 1 hour.
  2. Cook rice with whole spices until 80% done.
  3. In a pan, fry onions till golden. Add marinated chicken and cook halfway.
  4. Layer rice over chicken, sprinkle mint, coriander, and fried onions.
  5. Seal and cook on low flame for 20 mins (dum method).

Gulab Jamun

Author: Elizabeth

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Milk (as needed to knead)
  • Oil or ghee (for frying)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Cardamom and rose water (for syrup)

Instructions:

  1. Make syrup: boil sugar + water + cardamom until slightly sticky. Add rose water.
  2. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and add milk little by little to make soft dough.
  3. Make smooth small balls without cracks.
  4. Deep fry on low heat until golden brown.
  5. Soak in warm sugar syrup for 1 hour before serving.
